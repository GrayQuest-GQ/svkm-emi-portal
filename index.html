Important Instructions
Must-Follow Before Processing
Non-compliance may lead to application rejection or delays.
01
Enter the Student ID
Ensure the correct Student ID is mentioned in the Customer Portal while filling the EMI application. Missing or incorrect IDs will cause processing delays.
Required
02
Collect the Fee Structure
Always collect the official Fee Structure issued by the institute for the respective student. This is used as a mandatory validation document during processing.
Required
03
Validate Institute & Location ⚠️
Before submitting, carefully verify that the correct Institute and Location are selected in the Customer Portal. An incorrect selection may result in the disbursement being processed to the wrong institution — which is difficult to reverse and can cause significant delays.
Critical
